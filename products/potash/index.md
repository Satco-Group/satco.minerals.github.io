  <h1>Potash (Potassium Chloride - KCl)</h1>
  <p>Potash is a vital industrial and agricultural mineral, primarily composed of potassium chloride. It is recognized globally as the primary source of potassium, an essential nutrient for plant growth and a key chemical component in various industrial processes.</p>

  <h2>Key Properties</h2>
  <ul>
    <li><b>High Water Solubility:</b> Rapidly dissolves in aqueous solutions, ensuring efficient nutrient uptake or chemical integration.</li>
    <li><b>High Potassium Content:</b> Offers a concentrated source of K₂O (Potassium Oxide).</li>
    <li><b>Chemical Purity:</b> Refined to eliminate impurities that could interfere with delicate industrial processes.</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>Advantages & Disadvantages</h2>
  <ul>
    <li><b>Advantages:</b> Essential for crop yield optimization; highly effective as a flux in aluminum recycling.</li>
    <li><b>Disadvantages:</b> Highly hygroscopic; requires rigorous moisture-proof storage to prevent clumping.</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>Applications</h2>
  <ul>
    <li><b>Agriculture:</b> Used as a primary fertilizer to enhance crop resilience and yield.</li>
    <li><b>Industrial/Chemical:</b> Essential in the production of potassium hydroxide (KOH) and other potassium-based chemicals.</li>
    <li><b>Food Industry:</b> Utilized as a salt substitute and stabilizing agent.</li>
    <li><b>Metal Processing:</b> Serves as a flux in the smelting and recycling of aluminum.</li>
  </ul>
